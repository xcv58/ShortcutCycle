# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 26, 2026**

**ShortcutCycle** ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and handle your information when you use our application.

## 1. Data Collection

**We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data.**

ShortcutCycle is a localized macOS utility that operates entirely on your device.
*   **No Tracking**: We do not track your usage or usage patterns.
*   **No Analytics**: We do not use third-party analytics services.
*   **No Accounts**: You do not need to create an account to use the application.

## 2. Permissions

ShortcutCycle requests **Accessibility Permissions** solely to perform its core function: detecting keyboard shortcuts and switching between applications. This data is processed locally in real-time and is never stored or transmitted.
